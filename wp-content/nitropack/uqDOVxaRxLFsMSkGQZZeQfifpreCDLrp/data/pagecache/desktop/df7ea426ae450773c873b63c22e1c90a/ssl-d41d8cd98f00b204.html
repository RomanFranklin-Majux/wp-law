Get to Know Keith

Decades Working to Help People in Need

Keith Medved has been a trial lawyer for more than 40-years and has helped obtain verdicts and settlements totaling more than $50,000,000.

A native of Gary, Indiana, where he grew up in the 1960s, Keith has become a leading advocate for injury victims. After attending Purdue University and graduating from John Marshall law school, Keith established his practice in 1980 where he concentrated on representing individuals facing legal challenges. Keith was one of the first lawyers in Indiana to concentrate his practice on helping victims of nursing home abuse and neglect and is widely regarded as a go-to lawyer for elder abuse matters. Other lawyers regularly come to Keith for assistance with their nursing home abuse cases, including some of the largest firms in the Midwest.

Keith is also well-known for the high level of compassion and empathy he has for his clients.  While Keith’s built a strong reputation for his skills as an advocate, he is perhaps best known for the personal relationships he’s developed with injury victims and their families over the course of four decades. 

Did You Know?

Keith was an accomplished high school athlete and began his career as a sports announcer on broadcast radio before attending law school.
